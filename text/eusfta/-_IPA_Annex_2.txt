ANNEX 2 
 
 
LAND EXPROPRIATION 
 
1.  Notwithstanding Article 2.6 (Expropriation), where Singapore is the expropriating 
Party, any measure of expropriation relating to land, which shall be as defined in the 
Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 152)1, shall be upon payment of compensation at 
market value in accordance with the aforesaid legislation. 
 
2.  For the purposes of this Agreement, any measure of expropriation under the Land 
Acquisition Act (Chapter 152) should be for a public purpose or incidental to a public 
purpose. 
 
 
________________ 
 
                                                           
1  Land Acquisition Act (Chapter 152) as of the date of the entry into force of this Agreement.
