APPENDIX 2-A-2 
TARIFF SCHEDULE OF SINGAPORE 
 
 
1.  The  provisions  of  this  Schedule  are  expressed  in  terms  of  the  Singapore  Trade 
Classification, Customs and Excise Duties 2012 (“STCCE”), and the interpretation of 
the provisions of this Schedule, including the product coverage of subheadings of this 
Schedule, shall be governed by the General Notes, Section Notes, and Chapter Notes 
and subheading notes of the STCCE. To the extent that provisions of this Schedule 
are identical to the corresponding provisions of the STCCE, the provisions of this 
Schedule shall have the same meaning as the corresponding provisions of the STCCE. 
 
2.  The base rates of duties set out in this Schedule reflect Singapore’s MFN customs 
duty rates in effect on 1 January 2017. 
 
3.  Pursuant to Article 2.6 (Reduction and/or Elimination of Customs Duties on Imports), 
Singapore shall eliminate customs duties on all originating goods of Sri Lanka, except 
for products in Category “X”, in accordance with Singapore’s tariff schedule set out 
in this Appendix 2-A-2.
