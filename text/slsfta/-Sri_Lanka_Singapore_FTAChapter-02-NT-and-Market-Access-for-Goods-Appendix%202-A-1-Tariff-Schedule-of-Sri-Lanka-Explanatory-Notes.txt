APPENDIX 2-A-1 
TARIFF SCHEDULE OF SRI LANKA 
 
 
1. 
The provisions of this Schedule are expressed in terms of the National Customs Tariff 
Guide of Sri Lanka (NCTGSL), and the interpretation of the provisions of this Schedule, 
including the product coverage of subheadings of this Schedule, shall be governed by 
the General Notes, Section Notes, and Chapter Notes and subheading notes of the 
NCTGSL. To the extent that provisions of this Schedule are identical to the 
corresponding provisions of NCTGSL, the provisions of this Schedule shall have the 
same meaning as the corresponding provisions of the NCTGSL. 
 
2. 
The base rates of duties set out in this Schedule reflect Sri Lanka’s MFN Customs duty 
rates in effect on 31 December 2017. 
 
3. 
Pursuant to Article 2.6 (Reduction and /or Elimination of Customs Duties on Imports), 
Sri Lanka shall eliminate customs duties on all originating goods of Singapore, except 
for products in Category “X”, in accordance with Sri Lanka’s tariff schedule set out in 
this Appendix 2-A-1.
