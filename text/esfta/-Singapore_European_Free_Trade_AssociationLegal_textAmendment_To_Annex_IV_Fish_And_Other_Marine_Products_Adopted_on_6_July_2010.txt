DECISION OF THE JOINT EFTA-SINGAPORE COMMITTEE 
 
 
(Adopted on 6 July 2010) 
 
AMENDMENT TO ANNEX IV 
FISH AND OTHER MARINE PRODUCTS 
 
 
THE JOINT COMMITTEE, 
 
Having  regard  to  the  Free  Trade  Agreement  between  Singapore  and  the  EFTA  States 
(hereinafter referred to as “the Agreement”), 
 
Noting that there are technical errors in Annex IV Fish and Other Marine Products, some of 
which occurred through the adoption and application of the Joint Committee Decision No. 3 
of 2007, amending Annex IV Fish and Other Marine Products,  
 
Wishing to rectify these errors with the desire to clarify the meaning and increasing the 
harmonisation of the scope of Annex IV with other existing agreements, 
 
Having  regard  to  Article  55,  paragraph  8,  of  the  Agreement,  empowering  the  Joint 
Committee to amend Annexes to the Agreement
