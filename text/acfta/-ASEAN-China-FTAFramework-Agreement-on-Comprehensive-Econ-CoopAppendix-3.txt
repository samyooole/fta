Appendix 3 
                  Annex 2 
 
Specific Products Covered By The Early Harvest Programme Under 
Article 6(3)(a)(iii) 
 
A. Brunei and Singapore shall be parties to any arrangements that have been 
               
agreed  on  or  will  be  agreed  to  between  China  and  any  other  Party 
pursuant  to  Article  6(3)(a)(iii)  so  long  as  they  individually  provide 
written notification to China and the rest of the ASEAN Member States 
through  the  ASEAN  Secretariat  of  their  intended  effective  date  of 
participation in such arrangements. 
 
B.  Brunei and Singapore’s lists of specific products under this Annex shall 
be adminstratively inserted by the ASEAN Secretariat after China has 
verified the HS Codes and product descriptions in Brunei and Singapore’s 
lists. The ASEAN  Secretariat shall distribute an updated copy of this 
Annex to all Parties.
