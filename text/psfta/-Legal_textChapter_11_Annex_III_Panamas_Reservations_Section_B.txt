Financial services

Type of Reservation: 
National Treatment

Cross-border Trade in Financial Services 
Senior Management and Board of Directors  
 
Description: 
Panama reserves the right to adopt or maintain any 
measure dealing with pension fund services. 
 
Existing Measures: 
Law 10 of April 16, 1993, establishing incentives to set 
up funds and other benefits for retirees and pensioners. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
                                                                                                                                      
 
IIIB - PA -2
Sector: 
 
 
Financial Services 
 
Sub-sector:  
 
All sub-sectors 
 
Type of Reservation: 
Most-Favoured Nation Treatment 
 
Description: 
Panama reserves the right to adopt or maintain any 
measure that accord differential treatment to any 
country under any bilateral or multilateral international 
agreement between Panama and such country in force 
or signed prior to, on or after the date of entry into force 
of this Agreement.1 
 
 
 
 
                                                 
1 If a Panama enters into an agreement of the type referred to in the above paragraph, it shall, in 
future reviews of this Agreement pursuant to Article 17.1 (Administrative Commission of the 
Agreement), afford adequate opportunity to the other Party to negotiate the benefits therein.
