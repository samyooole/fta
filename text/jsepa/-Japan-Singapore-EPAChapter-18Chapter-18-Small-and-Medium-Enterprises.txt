The Parties shall likewise co-operate to facilitate investments of Singapore SMEs in Japan.Article 132Joint Committee on SMEs1.For the purposes of effective implementation of this Chapter, a JointCommittee on SMEs (hereinafter referred to in this Article as “the Committee”)shall be established.  The functions of the Committee shall be:(a)reviewing and discussing issues concerning the effectiveimplementation of this Chapter;(b)exchanging views and information on the promotion of SMEs co-operation;(c)identifying and recommending ways of further co-operationbetween the Parties; and(d)discussing other issues relating to SMEs co-operation.2.The composition of the Committee shall be specified in theImplementing Agreement.CHAPTER 19BROADCASTINGArticle 133Co-operation in the Field of BroadcastingThe Parties, recognising both the potential of broadcasting as a means forpromoting understanding between the Parties and the rapid development ofinnovative broadcasting services, shall encourage co-operation in the field ofbroadcasting between the Parties.
