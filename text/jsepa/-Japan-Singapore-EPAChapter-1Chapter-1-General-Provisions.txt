goods;(iii)promoting paperless trading between the Parties;(iv)facilitating the mutual recognition of the results ofconformity assessment procedures for products orprocesses;(v)removing barriers to trade in services between theParties;(vi)mutually enhancing investment opportunities andstrengthening protection for investors and investments;(vii)easing the movement of business people includingprofessionals;(viii)developing co-operation between the Parties in the fieldof intellectual property;(ix)enhancing opportunities in the government procurementmarket; and- Page 3
