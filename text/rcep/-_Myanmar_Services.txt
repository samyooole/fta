ANNEX II 
SCHEDULE OF SPECIFIC COMMITMENTS FOR SERVICES 
 
MYANMAR 
 
 
EXPLANATORY NOTES 
 
 
1.  The classification of services in this Schedule is based on the Services Sectoral Classification list of World Trade Organization Notification No. 
MTN.GNS/W/120 dated on 10 July 1991 and is in accordance with the definition in the 1991 UN Provisional Central Product Classification (CPC), 
unless otherwise specified. 
 
2.  For greater certainty, in accordance with paragraph 4 of Article 8.3 (Scheduling of Commitments), Myanmar is not making commitments under Article 
8.6 (Most-Favoured-Nation Treatment), and Article 8.10 (Transparency List). In accordance with paragraph 5 of Article 8.7 (Schedules of Specific 
Commitments), Myanmar is not making commitments under paragraph 3 of Article 8.7 (Schedules of Specific Commitments). 
 
3.  Unbound* means unbound due to lack of technical feasibility.
