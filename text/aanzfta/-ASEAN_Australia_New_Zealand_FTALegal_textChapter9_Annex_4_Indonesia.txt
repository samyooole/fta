INDONESIA’S SCHEDULE OF MOVEMENT OF NATURAL PERSONS COMMITMENTS 
AANZFTA – Annex 4 (Indonesia) 
  
 
1.  Indonesia’s  commitments  under  Chapter  9  (Movement  of  Natural  Persons),  and  under  Article  3  (National 
Treatment) and Article 4 (Market Access) of the Trade in Services Chapter, in relation to the supply by a service 
supplier of one Party through presence of natural persons of a Party in the territory of another Party apply only in 
relation to the categories of persons set out below. 
 
2.  In accordance with Articles 3, 4 and 8 (National Treatment, Market Access and Specific Commitments) of the 
Trade in Services Chapter, for the categories of persons set out in this Schedule, Indonesia specifies below any terms, 
conditions, limitations or qualifications in relation to the supply of a service by a service supplier of a Party through the 
presence of natural persons of a Party in the territory of Indonesia
