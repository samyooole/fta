Intra-corporate transferees   Definition: An executive, manager or a specialist:   (a) who is an employee of a goods supplier, service supplier or investor of a Party with a commercial presence in New Zealand; and  (b) whose salary and any related payments are paid entirely by the service supplier or enterprise that employs the intra-corporate transferee.  Executives:   Definition: A natural person of a Party who primarily directs the management of an enterprise, exercises wide latitude in decision making and receives only general supervision or direction from higher level executives, the board of directors or stockholders of the enterprise.
