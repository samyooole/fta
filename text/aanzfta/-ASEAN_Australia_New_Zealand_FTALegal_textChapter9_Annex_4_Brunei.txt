1.    Brunei Darussalam’s commitments under Chapter 9 (Movement 
of  Natural  Persons)  and  under  Article  3  (National  Treatment)  and

relation to the categories of persons set out below.

Market Access and Specific Commitments) of the Trade in Services 
Chapter, for the categories of persons set out in this Schedule, Brunei 
Darussalam  specifies  below  any  terms,  conditions,  limitations  or 
qualifications in relation to the supply of a service by a service supplier 
of a Party through the presence of natural persons of a Party in the 
territory of Brunei Darussalam.
