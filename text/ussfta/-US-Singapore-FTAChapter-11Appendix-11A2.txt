    APPENDIX 11A.2   PROFESSION      Disaster Relief Claims Adjuster              Management Consultant     131    MINIMUM EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS AND ALTERNATIVE CREDENTIALS    Baccalaureate Degree, and successful completion of training in the appropriate areas of insurance adjustment pertaining to disaster relief claims; or three years experience in claims adjustment and successful completion of training in the appropriate areas of insurance adjustment pertaining to disaster relief claims.     Baccalaureate Degree.  If the degree is in a discipline not related to the area of the consulting agreement, then equivalent professional experience as established by statement or professional credential attesting to three years experience in a field or specialty related to the consulting agreement is required.
