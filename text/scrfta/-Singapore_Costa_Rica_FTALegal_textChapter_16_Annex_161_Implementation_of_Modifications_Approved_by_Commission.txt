Annex 16.1 
Implementation of Modifications Approved by the Commission 
 
 
In the case of Costa Rica, decisions of the Commission under Article 
16.1.3(b) (The Free Trade Commission) will be equivalent to the instrument 
referred to in Article 121.4, third paragraph (Protocolo de Menor Rango) of the 
Constitución Política de la República de Costa Rica.
