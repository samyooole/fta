Appendix 5 
Services (other than Construction Services) 
 
This Chapter applies to all services procured by the entities listed in Appendices 
1, 2 and 3, subject to the Notes to the respective Appendices and the General 
Notes, except for the services excluded in this Appendix. All services covered 
by this Appendix are subject to the measures listed in Costa Rica’s Schedules 
to  Annex  I  and  II  (Non-Conforming  Measures)  for  Chapter  10  (Trade  in 
Services) and Chapter 11 (Investment) on the basis that it does not derogate 
from or take away the Services commitments made under this Appendix by 
Costa Rica to Singapore.

Construction Services 
 
This Chapter applies to all construction services procured by the entities listed 
in Appendices 1, 2 and 3, subject to the Notes to the respective Appendices 
and the General Notes. All construction services covered by this Appendix are

Construction Services commitments made under this Appendix by Costa Rica 
to Singapore. 
 
 
Appendix 7 
General Notes 
 
Unless  otherwise  specified  herein,  the  following  General  Notes  apply  to  all 
Appendices of this Annex. 
 
1.  Use of lists of suppliers: 
 
Entities listed in Appendices 1 and 3 might use lists of suppliers. 
 
2.   Limited tendering: 
 
Notwithstanding Article 8.14 (Limited Tendering Procedures), procuring entities 
listed in Appendices 1, 2 and 3 might award contracts by limited tendering 
procedures in the following circumstances:  
 
(a)  If it is strictly necessary for reasons of public interest, duly proven.  
 
(b)  For objects involving qualified security. 
 
(c)  In cases of urgent attention of judicial procedures. 
 
(d)  For conciliation and arbitration services. 
 
(e)  In  cases  of  building  and  establishment  of  government  offices 
located abroad, as well as procurement of foreign physical or legal 
persons that will provide their services abroad.  
Annex 8.1 - 6 
    
 
 
3.  Specific exclusions 
 
This Chapter shall not apply to any procurement made by a covered entity on 
behalf of a non covered entity.
