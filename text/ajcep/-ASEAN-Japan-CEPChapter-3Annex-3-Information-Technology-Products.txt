Annex 3 
Information Technology Products 
 
  A good which is covered by Attachment A or B of the 
Ministerial Declaration on Trade in Information Technology 
Products adopted in the Ministerial Conference of the World 
Trade Organization on 13 December 1996 and is used as a 
material in the production of another good in a Party may 
be considered as an originating material of the Party, 
regardless of the applicable product specific rule for the 
former good, provided that the former good is assembled in 
any Party, except where the former good is classified under 
subheadings 8541.10 through 8542.90.
