 
 
JAPAN’S SCHEDULE OF COMMITMENTS FOR 
TEMPORARY ENTRY FOR BUSINESS PERSONS 
 
 
1.  The following sets out Japan’s commitments in accordance with Article 12.4 (Grant 
of Temporary Entry) in respect of the temporary entry of business persons. 
 
2.  Japan extends its commitments under this Annex to all Parties regardless of the 
categories offered by the other Parties. 
 
3.  For the purposes of this schedule, the term “CPC” means the Provisional Central 
Product  Classification  (Statistical  Papers  Series  M  No.  77,  Department  of  International 
Economic and Social Affairs, Statistical Office of the United Nations, New York, 1991).
